JELLOF RICE RECIPE

INGREDIENTS NEEDED TO MAKE YUMMY JELLOF RICE

Jellof rice is one of nigerias delicacy. it is cooked in every home and can be done by every adult Nigerian. On this note, we can say it is a multi-cultural food. Notable ingrediennts used to make jellof rice are as follows:

    rice
    beef/chicken/fish
    balls of fresh tomatoes or tomatoes paste
    onion balls
    spicy pepper
    seasoning
    salt to taste
    cooking oil
    spices for taste and others as desired

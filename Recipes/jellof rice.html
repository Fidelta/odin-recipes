JELLOF RICE RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

Jellof rice is one of nigerias delicacy. it is cooked in every home and can be done by every adult Nigerian. On this note, we can say it is a multi-cultural food. Both natives and foreigners enjoy eating Nigerian Jellof Rice. ot is the most sold food in most eateries and food vending sites in Nigeria

there have been argument, comparing Nigerian Jellof and Ghana Jellof. Well, i can't surely choose for you but i can convince you that there can never be a complete Nigerian "party" and "sunday" without our dearest jellof rice gracing our tables and satisfying our cravings

INGREDIENTS NEEDED TO MAKE YUMMY JELLOF RICE

    Rice
    Beef/chicken/fish
    Balls of fresh tomatoes or tomatoes paste
    Onion balls
    Spicy pepper
    Seasoning
    Salt to taste
    Cooking oil
    Spices for taste and other ingredients as desired

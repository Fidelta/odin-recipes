NIGERIAN JELLOF RICE

DESCRIPTION

Jellof rice is one of nigerias delicacy. it is cooked in every home and can be done by every adult Nigerian. On this note, we can say it is a multi-cultural food. Both natives and foreigners enjoy eating Nigerian Jellof Rice. ot is the most sold food in most eateries and food vending sites in Nigeria

there have been argument, comparing Nigerian Jellof and Ghana Jellof. Well, i can't surely choose for you but i can convince you that there can never be a complete Nigerian "party" and "sunday" without our dearest jellof rice gracing our tables and satisfying our cravings

INGREDIENTS NEEDED TO MAKE YUMMY JELLOF RICE

In order to make yummy Nigerian Jellof, the following ingredients are required

  • Rice
  • Beef/Chicken stock
  • Fresh Tomatoes Ball or Tomatoes Paste
  • Onion Balls
  • Red bell Peppers
  • Seasoninigs
  • Salt to Taste
  • Cooking/vegetable Oil
  • Fresh Ginger and Garlic (optional)
  • Bay leaves
  • Spices like curry powder, dried thyme, and others for taste/aroma and any other additional ingredient as desired

PROCEDURE FOR MAKING YUMMY JELLOF RICE

Jellof rice can be made in a number of ways depending on who is making it and for what occassion. My preferred, simple step by step procedure is as presented:

  1. Parboil your proteins, the rice for like 5 to 10 minutes and remove from water
  2. Blend your red pepper ball, tomatoes onions, ginger and garlic and cook to dry the water in them
  3. Using your cooking pan/pot, fry your proteins then put in little quantity of cooking oil depending on the quantity of rice and fry your chopped onions, pepper and tomatoes
  4. After frying, put in the required quantity of water and allow to boil
  5. Add your spices and other ingredients, mix well then add your rice, stir well to mix and allow to cook for some minutes not under too much heat
  6. Check for when its done. Cover with a tight lid and leave to simmer for about 5 mins then serve hot

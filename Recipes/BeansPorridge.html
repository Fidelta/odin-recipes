BEANS PORRIDGE

DESCRIPTION

Nigerian Beans porridge is made with the combination of beans, vegetables, and floavourful spices, this dish offers a delighful blend of textures and flavours.

Nigerian beans porridge is the definition of complete comfort and can be paired with garri, bread or ogi (fermented corn starch porridge).

LIST OF INGREDIENTS FOR COOKING BEANS

  • Honey beans/Nigerian brown beans
  • Palm oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Onion
  • Ground dried shrimp/cray fish (optional)
  • Salt
  • Shrimp bullion

INSTRUCTIONS ON HOW TO COOK

  1. Rince the beans and place in a deep pot of water.
  2. Boil the beans on medium heat until it becomes soft, it could take about 40 minutes.
  3. While the beans are boiling, blend the tomatoes, peppers and onions and set aside.
  4. Once the beans are softened from boiling (do not drain the water), turn the heat down to low, then por in the blended tomatoes into the pot.
  5. Allow the beans to cook for an additional 10 minutes with the tomatoes, then add in the crayfish, bullion and palm oil, stir and cook for another 10 minutes
  6. Season the beans with salt to your taste. Turn off the heat, and allow to sit fot 5 minutes before serving

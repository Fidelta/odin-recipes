EGG SAUCE

DESCRIPTION

Egg sauce together with boiled yam is one of the breakfast staples in Nigeria. Egg sauce can also be consumed with white rice or bread

Egg sauce is very easy to make. Typically in Nigeria, we use tomatoes, scotch bonnet chilli and onions in making this sauce.

INGREDIENTS FOR MAKING EGG SAUCE

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Scotched bonnet, chopped
  • Red bell pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Curry

DIRECTIONS ON HOW TO MAKE EGG SAUCE

  1. Break the eggs into a shallow bowl, whisk till egg white and egg yolk are all well combined
  2. Place a frying pan on a medium/low heat, add vegetable oil, heat for about 2-3 minutes
  3. Add chopped peppers and onions and cok for about 4-5 minutes or till oil floats on the sauce. add salt, seasoning, curry and thyme. stir to mix all the ingredients together with sauce and to avoid stciky pan
  4. Add whisked eggs to sauce and leave for about a minute.
  5. mix well and serve
